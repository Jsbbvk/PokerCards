Rules
Each player is dealt one card face down. This card is hidden to everyone except for that player.
Each player is then dealt one card face up. This card is visible to everyone.

Players will have an option to HIT or STAND.
To HIT means to be dealt another card face up.
To STAND means that the player is satisfied with the cards they have and do not want to be dealt more cards.

The objective of this game is to have the sum of the values of all the cards you have to be as close to 21 or exactly 21.
However, going over 21 means a BUST and you automatically lose.

Each FACE CARD counts as 10 and an ACE can either be 1 or 11, and this value is interchangeable anytime.

Once you are satisfied with your cards, press STAND.

Once everyone STANDS or BUSTS, the person (or people in case of ties) with the sum of the cards' values closest to 21 wins.
